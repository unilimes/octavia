A blinking eye lingers between hazy reminiscences and hidden childhood secrets. Octavia and her fetish idol become fields for exploration.

Octavia is an interactive film where the viewer can explore a sensory narration and delve deeper in the interaction between the subliminal elements of the story and its characters. A simulation of the blink of an eye, the manipulation of a sphere, hidden sounds are all fragments entangled in the experience of Octavia.

Attempting to simulate and exasperate human senses is a direction we will consider to explore further in future VR and interactive projects.

TECHNOLOGY WebGL, Javascript, ThreeJS, Web Audio API

This film runs with WebGL web technology and brings a mix of video, 3D, real time video effects and dynamic audio to create a feeling of fractured time, continuous deformation and multiple levels of view.
